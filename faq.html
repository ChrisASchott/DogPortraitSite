Got questions?

Are these printed?

No, these are original paintings on stretched canvas.


How large are these portraits?

Each portrait measures 4 inches square.


How long does this take?

Paintings are usually completed within a week of ordering. Different shipping options are available.


Do I get a preview?

Yes, we email an image for approval before sending the final portrait.


What kind of paint?

These portraits are painted with acrylic paint.


Will the painting last?

Yes, acrylic is pretty tough. Additionally, all paintings are coated with a matte UV protectant varnish.


Do these come framed?

No, each painting comes with an appropriately sized, wooden display easel.


Do you paint other animals?

Not usually, but we can talk.


Can you include a person?

No, we specialize in dog portraits.


Can I get an action shot?

I'm sorry, we specialize in faces.
